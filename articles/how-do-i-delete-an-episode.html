Note: If you are hosting your podcast with Alitu, deleting an episode from your dashboard will remove that episode from your RSS feed.

  1. First, click the 'trash' icon to the right of any episode in your Episode Listing that you'd like to delete.


    ​

  2. Click 'remove' from the pop-up to confirm that you want to delete this episode and it will be removed. Please note that this step cannot be undone so be sure to think twice before confirming the removal.
