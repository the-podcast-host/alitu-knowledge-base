Resetting your Password

We don't have access to the password that you set for your Alitu account, but it's very simple to reset this.

  1. Visit the Alitu login page:

    https://app.alitu.com/login

  2. Click on the 'Forgot your password?' link:

  3. You will receive an email in about 5 minutes to your Alitu email address. Please be sure to check your SPAM/JUNK folder if this email hasn't arrived in 10 minutes.
    ​

Let us know if you have any questions, we're available through chat using the button in the lower right corner of your screen. 😊
