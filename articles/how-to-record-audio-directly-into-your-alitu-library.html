If you want to create a quick voice clip, you can record directly into the Alitu library and use that voice clip in your intros, promos, etc.

To get started, click on My Library:

Then click on 'Add to Library':

In the pop-up window click 'Record Audio'. Give your new voice recording a name and click 'Start' to begin recording:

If a pop-up appears, click Allow to let your browser use your microphone.

Once you're done, click 'Stop'
​

Finally, click 1) the play icon to listen to your recording, 2) click Re-Record to start over, or 3) click Save to save your new voice recording.
​

You now have a new voice clip in your Library that you can use in podcast episodes.
