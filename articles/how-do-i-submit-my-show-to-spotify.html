Important. If you're moving from another host to Alitu, DO NOT submit your podcast to Spotify again as this will result in duplicate listings. Please take a look at how to set up a 301 redirect instead.

Before you start, you need to have at least one published episode in Alitu to submit your show to Spotify.

  1. Visit the Alitu dashboard under Settings > Podcast Distribution or visit this direct link https://creators.spotify.com/addpodcast to Spotify's Submission portal.

  2. Log in with your Spotify account details, or choose SIGN UP if you don’t have a Spotify account yet.

  3. Once you’re signed in, click Find an existing show from this page
    ​

  4. Under 'Where is your show hosted?' choose 'Somewhere else'

  5. Paste in your Alitu RSS feed link and click 'Next'
    ​

  6. On the next screen, click Send code and Spotify will send a verification email to the address in your RSS feed. Copy the 8-digit code from that email and paste in the submit form.
    ​

  7. Add podcast info like the category, language, and country.

  8. Review the info then click SUBMIT
    ​

Once you click SUBMIT, Spotify will provide your podcast’s Spotify URL. It can take a couple of days for your podcast to show in the app and search results.

Alitu’s Statistics page does not include Spotify listens or downloads. This is because Spotify uploads your podcast directly to their servers and does not pass-back the information to Alitu. So, we don’t have those downloads data unless Spotify shares that information with us.

You can access your Spotify statistics in your Spotify for Creators account.
