Once your episode is complete, you can publish the episode directly to your Podbean account. You can decide whether or not you want your episode published as a draft or as a live episode.
​

Integrate your Alitu Account with your Podbean Account

  1. On the publish page, click 'Set up my show':

  2. Click 'I use Podbean':
    ​

  3. Log into your Podbean account using the pop-up window:
    ​

    If you're already logged into Podbean, select the name of your Podcast channel in the window and click 'Authorize Now':
    ​

  4. On your publish page, you can now click 'Send to Podbean':
    ​

  5. Choose whether you want to upload and save as a draft or upload and publish as Public or Premium.
    ​

That's it. Your episode will be uploaded directly to your Podbean dashboard.
​
Note: If you ever see the message, "Podbean Access Token Expired", you simply need to disconnect and re-connect your Podbean account.
