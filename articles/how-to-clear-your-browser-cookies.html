Browsers such as Chrome save data from websites in their cache and cookies. Clearing your browser cache and cookies fixes certain problems, like loading episodes and pages, error messages, or formatting issues on websites. This also ensures that your browser has the latest update.

Here’s how to clear cookies for Alitu in Chrome:

1. Go to your Alitu page.

2. Click the lock icon from your browser

3. Choose ‘Cookies and site data’ from the dropdown and then choose 'Manage cookies and site data'.

4. A pop-up bar window will appear. Make sure alitu.com, production.api.alitu.com, and app.alitu.com cookies were removed by clicking the trash icon and then clicking ‘Done’.

You’re all set!

If this didn’t help try clearing your browser cache or reach out to our support team using the chat box below. Thanks!
