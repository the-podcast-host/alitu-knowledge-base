Why have a Podcast Website?

Having a website for your podcast is important, especially for listeners who prefer to listen directly from a website. A podcast website enhances your online visibility, boosts SEO, and provides a home for your show on the internet that you fully own and control. Alitu offers a simple website for your podcast, featuring all the essential information about your show including:

  • Podcast Name

  • Podcast Artwork

  • Podcast Description

  • Podcast Episodes

  • Directory links

  • Social links

  • Website link

How to set up your Alitu Website

The free podcast website is available for Alitu-hosted shows only.

Navigate to Show Settings >> Show Website and click 'Create my Free Podcast Website' and then follow the steps below:

  1. Enter a subdomain for your show. This subdomain will be followed by alitu.com so it will look something like this, https://subdomain.alitu.com where your actual subdomain will show in place of the word, subdomain. Click the 'Claim' button.

  2. It can take up to 24 hours for your subdomain to become active.

  3. You can add links to your podcast on Apple & Spotify. If your show is new and you don't have links to these pages yet, skip these for now.

  4. You can continue filling in the social media links, as well as a link to your personal website if you have one, and then click 'Save settings'.

  5. Then, add your Website Footer Text which is any legal text that you want to add, usually your name or the name of your company, and click, 'Save settings'.

  6. You can check the #MadeWithAlitu button and a badge will appear on your website.
