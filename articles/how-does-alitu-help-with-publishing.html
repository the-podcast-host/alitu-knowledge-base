You will need to choose a media host for your podcast. The host will provide your RSS feed and make sure your episodes are available to your audience.

Alitu offers podcast hosting which you can activate easily right inside your dashboard at no additional cost. Each account receives 1,000 downloads per month at the free level.
​
However, Alitu also integrates with the most popular third-party podcasts hosts. Through this integration you can publish your podcast episodes directly to your integrated host without having to download your final files and then upload them to the host.

As of this date, you can publish your podcast episodes from Alitu directly to:

  • Alitu Hosting

  • Buzzsprout

  • Captivate

  • Castos

  • Libsyn

  • Podbean

  • Spreaker

  • Transistor

​If you don’t have a podcast host yet, make sure to check out a full rundown of the best podcast hosting options here.
