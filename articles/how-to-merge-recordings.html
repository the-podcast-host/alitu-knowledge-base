You can easily merge two or more files (they must be the exact same length) when you're uploading them to Alitu. This is ideal if you use something like Zoom to record your podcast interviews and you end up with 2 or more files, 1 for each voice.

  1. Create your new podcast episode then on the Upload Screen, either drag and drop your files to be merged, or click to add them to the episode:

  2. Click the merge icon to the right of both files to link them together & then click Add to Library:


    ​

    While the files are uploading they will be processed separately and then merged together for you to work on in the Episode Builder.

  3. Click 'Edit' to begin editing the Merged Files as your normally would:
    ​
