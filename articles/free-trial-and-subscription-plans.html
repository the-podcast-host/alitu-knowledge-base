Your Alitu Free Trial

The Alitu free trial is a great opportunity for you to test out the features of our podcast maker software to see how it will fit into your podcast workflow.

If you become a monthly or annual subscriber after your free trial you will have access to:

  • Unlimited number of episodes (check Fair Usage policy)

  • Unlimited file storage

  • Unlimited solo and remote studio recordings

  • Editing tool (waveform and text editing)

  • Magic filters

  • Royalty-Free music

  • 12 hours transcription per monthly billing period

  • AI assistance for your episode title and description

  • Free hosting for 1,000 downloads (optional)

  • And a lot more!
