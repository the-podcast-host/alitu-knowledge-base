1. In the editor tool bar, select 'Background music'.

2. You can choose music from our Free Music Library or upload your own music file to your library. To add your selected music file to the episode click 'Add to Episode'

3. Your music file will be added below the waveform.

4.. Click and drag to adjust the length of the music file.

5. Adjust the volume by hovering over the music file and sliding the volume bar. When you are happy, click 'Done'

6. To add a fade to your music and avoid abrupt starts or ends, hover over the beginning or end of the music track. The fade option will appear. Click and drag to adjust the fade in/out."

7. To cancel the background music click 'Remove'
