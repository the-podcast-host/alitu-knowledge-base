  3. If you are on a Mac, you may see a follow-up image, letting you know about the permissions you just gave. Just click OK.

At the time of this article, Safari is not supported for recording in Alitu. If you're trying to record with the Safari browser, please use Chrome or Firefox.

How do I test my microphone?

If you have completed these steps and are not sure if your microphone is working, you can use this mic test:
