The Create Video feature produces a video version of a podcast episode. This video will contain a still image with an overlay of a dynamic soundwave that moves with the audio.

The resulting file is something that you can share to YouTube or other video sites to repurpose your show or promote your show on social media.

  1. On the publish page, click 'Create video':
    ​

  2. Alitu then goes to work creating a video version of your episode:
    ​

  3. Once the video is created, you can return to your publish screen to download the video:


That's it! The video file can be shared anywhere you choose.

Heads up! If you want to play back the audio on your computer, we recommend using a VLC player. QuickTime Player does not like mp3 audio streams in a mp4 container so there's no audio when you listen using that tool. You can download a VLC player using this link https://www.videolan.org/vlc/.
