Important. If you're moving from another host to Alitu, DO NOT submit your podcast to directories again as this will result in duplicate listings. Please take a look at how to set up a 301 redirect instead.

We have a Distribution section that helps you submit your podcast to some directories.

Simply click on the directory submission links, and you'll be redirected to the right page and then start submitting your podcast. Here are the steps you can follow for submission:

Submit to Pandora

  1. Go to https://www.ampplaybook.com/podcasts and click the 'Get Started' button

  2. Submission to Pandora requires a Simplecast account so you need to sign up or login to your Simplecast account.

  3. Once you've confirmed your account, go to this link https://connect.simplecast.com/

  4. Click the 'add show' button and paste your RSS feed and click 'Yes'.

  5. An email will be sent to your podcast email to confirm your feed.

  6. Once confirmed, your podcast will appear on the Pandora platform.

Submit to iHeartRadio

  1. Go to https://podcasters.iheart.com/#submit-podcast and choose 'Submit Your Podcast'.

  2. Log in or create an account if you don't have one yet.

  3. Click 'Add Your Podcast' button

  4. Paste in your RSS feed and click 'Verify Podcast RSS'

  5. Agree to iHeartRadio's Terms & Conditions and then click Submit Podcast.

  6. A verification email will be sent to your podcast email address to claim and complete the submission.

  7. Your podcast will appear on their platform within 24 hours.

Submit to Deezer

  1. Go to https://podcasters.deezer.com/ and click the 'Publish My Podcast' button.

  2. Paste in your RSS feed and click 'Verify Podcast RSS'

  3. A verification code will be sent to your podcast email. Copy that and enter the code on Deezer then click Next.

  4. Fill out the form about your podcast then click 'submit'.

  5. All done. Your podcast is on Deezer!
