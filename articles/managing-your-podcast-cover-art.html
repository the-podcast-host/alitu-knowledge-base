For Alitu Hosting users only.

If you do not host your podcast with Alitu you need to manage your podcast cover art directly with your chosen host.

Uploading or updating your podcast artwork

  1. Your podcast artwork should be a square file, 3000 x 3000 pixels, in jpeg or png format, and under 5MB in size.

  2. Navigate to Show Settings in your Alitu dashboard and click on 'Show Details'

  3. Click 'Upload new' to select your file from where it's stored on your computer
    ​

  4. You should see 'All changes saved' at the bottom of the screen.
    ​

Design or edit artwork using Adobe

You can now edit existing artwork or design artwork from scratch using the Adobe plugin inside of Alitu.

  1. The adobe editor is pre-set for the correct dimensions for your podcast cover art

  2. You will need an adobe account to use this plugin, you can create an account for free.

  3. For specific help with the the features of the adobe plugin please read their user guide provided by Adobe.

  4. When you have finished creating/editing, simply press "Save" and the file will be uploaded into your Show Details settings.
