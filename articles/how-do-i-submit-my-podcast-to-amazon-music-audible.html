Are you moving from another host to Alitu? If so, DO NOT submit your podcast to directories again as this will result in duplicate entries. See how to set up a 301 redirect instead.

Amazon Music for podcasters is where you will submit and manage your podcast on Amazon Music.

You need to have at least one published episode in Alitu before you can submit your RSS feed to Amazon Music.

  1. Go to the Amazon Music for Podcasters home page and click Add or Claim your Podcast

  2. Sign in with your Amazon ID or create a new account

  3. Paste in your Alitu RSS feed and click submit

    You'll find your RSS feed in Alitu Settings > Podcast Distribution tab

4. Amazon Music will pull your show's information from the RSS feed. Check that the information is correct and click Confirm Email

5. Amazon Music will send a verification code to the email address linked to your show. Enter the code on the next screen and you’re all set!


Your show should appear on Amazon Music & Audible within 24 hours.
