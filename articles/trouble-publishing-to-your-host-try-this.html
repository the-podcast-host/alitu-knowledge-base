Are you having trouble publishing to your podcast host?

From time to time you may need to refresh the connection between Alitu and your podcast host. This doesn't mean anything is 'wrong', code changes, tokens expire, etc.
​
If you are having trouble publishing to your host, you can try refreshing the connection between Alitu and your podcast host.

Disconnect then Reconnect your podcast host

To disconnect your hosting, go to the Show Settings section, choose Show Hosting, and click the 'Disconnect' button

You can then re-connect to your host using the instructions you'll find here:
​

Publishing Your Episode
