Podcasting can feel overwhelming sometimes because there's so much to learn and do. Alitu can do a lot of the heavy lifting and save you time and money. If you ever get stuck using the software, our Success Team wants to help. Some of the ways we can help are below.

Office Hours

Every week we hold an online Office Hours Session, where one of our customer support team members will be answering any questions you have about Alitu. Please note this is like a drop in session and other users may be present. You can book Office Hours right inside the Podcraft with Alitu Community.

Book Office hours

One to One Support Sessions

We also have several slots each week where you can book some 1-1 time with one of our customer success team members. The sessions are 15 minutes along and can help guide you through Alitu or you can just ask us lots of questions to help you get on your podcasting journey. Book a 1:1 inside the Podcraft with Alitu Community

Chat with Us

You can contact support at any time using the chat bubble in the bottom right corner of your screen. Our Customer Success team is based in the UK and the US. We are available Monday - Friday during local time work hours (9am-5pm GMT/EST). If it is outside of our support times, we will get back to asap the next working day.

You can also email us at help@alitu.com

The Podcraft with Alitu Online Community

The Podcraft Community is included with every Alitu subscription and exists to provide a safe space for podcasters to make connections and get the help and encouragement they need to grow their podcasts without the icky sales posts, unreliable information, and post-and-ghost behaviours you see in some other groups. There is none of that here, just useful information and community. You should receive an invite to the community via email, but reach out to the support team if you need another link.
