Managing Multiple Shows

Previously if you wanted to manage multiple shows on Alitu, you had to create multiple accounts. Now, we have released a new option that enables you to manage more than one podcast from one Alitu account.

How to Move a Podcast from One Alitu Account to Another

To get both of your podcasts into one Alitu account, you'll need to import the 2nd podcast into the first account, then delete the 2nd account.

1. Create a new podcast in the account you want to be the main account:

2. Navigate to Show Settings >> Host Settings and select your podcast host for your 2nd podcast (In this example, we've chosen Alitu)

3. Add the details/information for your 2nd podcast here:

4. Next, navigate to Import Show and add the RSS feed from your 2nd podcast (from the account you want to move into this account):

5. After you make sure your 2nd podcast imported correctly in the first account, navigate to Show Settings >> Show Distribution and copy your newly-created podcast's RSS feed URL:

6. Then navigate to the 2nd account (with your 2nd podcast in it) and create a 301 redirect on the Show Settings >> Show Hosting screen:

7. Once your 2nd podcast has been moved into your first account and the redirect has been set, please log ingo the 2nd account once more and cancel the account for the 2nd podcast from your My Account dashboard.

Please reach out using the chat button in the bottom right corner if you need any help.
