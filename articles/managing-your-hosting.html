Selecting your host

You can set up your preferred hosting for your podcast in your dashboard at "Show Settings" >> "Show Hosting".

Click the button underneath the host you want to use and then follow the steps to set up your podcast hosting.

Which 3rd-Party Hosts does Alitu Integrate With

How to host directly with Alitu

Allow Alitu to be your one-stop shop for your podcast by activating Alitu Hosting in your "Show Hosting" section. Click the 'Go live with Alitu for free' button and when you publish, your episodes will be hosted right here with Alitu. Alitu Hosting is free for up to 1000 downloads per month.

How to upgrade Alitu hosting to a paid hosting plan

Note: If you are already an Alitu subscription customer and you add Alitu paid hosting part way through your current billing cycle, then you will be instantly charged the pro-rated hosting amount up till the date of your next billing cycle.

  1. Visit Show Hosting and click on 'Update hosting plan' as shown above.

  2. Next select the monthly paid plan you prefer

From this point on you will be charged your normal Alitu subscription price + the Alitu hosting add-on amount on your normal billing date.

You're set! Start publishing your episodes :)
