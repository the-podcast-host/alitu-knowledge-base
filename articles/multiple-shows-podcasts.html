Overview

You can now have more than one show / podcast inside Alitu. We have made it easy to add and manage your extra shows in Alitu.
​

You will have 1 Alitu login no matter how many extra shows you add. Each show will have it's own independent space for you to record, edit, store your files in the library and host using Alitu.

How much does an additional show cost?

Each new show or podcast you add will incur an additional cost to your regular subscription.

This will be in addition to your regular subscription costs + any other extra costs you may have (such as additional hosting downloads)

What's Included

Everything that you currently get from your primary subscription.

Having another show gives you all the same benefits that you get from your primary show. There are no "shared" limits **.

You get all the audio processing, unlimited episodes, 12 hours of transcription, recording, unlimited editing, magic filters and hosting.

**If hosting with Alitu and you have more than 1 show then your initial free download hosting limit will be 5000 downloads per month across ALL shows.

How to add an extra show

  1. Click on the button that says "My First Show" from the left hand side menu.

  2. Then select "add a new show"

  3. Confirm the additional show purchase by pressing "add to subscription"

When you add a new show, you will want to think about hosting and distribution. You can host inside Alitu but you will need to set this up and also any places you want to distribute your podcast too. Below are some articles that guide you through this.

How to switch between shows

  1. Click on the "Switch Shows" button from the left hand menu

  2. Select the show you want to switch to.

How to cancel a Show

You are able to cancel an individual show without cancelling your whole subscription.

If you host with Alitu for the show you want to cancel, then you should follow the steps for moving your podcast hosting your new host BEFORE you cancel.

If you want to cancel your whole subscription see our "Cancel Subscription" article

To cancel a show (but keep your main subscription active) you need to

  1. Navigate to and click the "switch shows" button

  2. Click the trash icon on the show you want to cancel/stop

  3. If you want to go ahead then confirm by "delete my show"

  4. After you have cancelled your show you will still have access up to the next billing date.
