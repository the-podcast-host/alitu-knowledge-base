Your podcast host will submit your episodes to the podcast directories for you.

Your podcast host will create and manage your RSS feed. The RSS feed is what the directories use to keep your show listing up-to-date.

Alitu helps prepare your final episode and then you publish the file to your podcast host. Right now, we have the hosting tool in beta mode. Check it out here https://help.alitu.com/en/collections/3346415-alitu-hosting
