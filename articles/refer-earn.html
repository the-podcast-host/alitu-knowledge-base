Share Alitu with your friends and network, and you'll earn $25 if your referral becomes a customer. Your referrals will get 25% off their Alitu subscription for the first three months.

How to copy and share your unique referral link

  1. In the left-hand sidebar of your Alitu dashboard, click Refer & Earn $25.

2. Copy your unique invite link
A pop-up will appear with your personal referral link. You can:

  • Copy the invite link directly, or

  • Share it using the built-in email or social buttons.

    What they get

    Anyone who signs up using your link receives 25% off their Alitu subscription for 3 months.

    What you get

    For every new customer who subscribes through your link, you earn $25.


How to add your payment details

At this time, you must have a PayPal account to be paid for your referrals. The rewards will be sent directly to this account.

Click the Rewards tab in the Refer & Earn pop-up, then:

  1. Select your country.

  2. Enter the email address linked to your PayPal account.

  3. Add your address.

  4. Choose whether you are registering as an individual or a business.

  5. Read and agree to the Terms & Conditions.

  6. Save your payment method.
