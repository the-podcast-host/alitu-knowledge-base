​It's simplest if you sign into your Castos account in another browser window before you begin this setup.

Integrate your Alitu Account with your Castos Account

  1. Click on 'Show Settings', select 'Show Hosting' and click on 'I use Castos':
    ​

  2. Navigate to the browser window where you're logged into Castos. Once there, Click on your initials and then click on 'Integrations':
    ​
    ​

  3. In Integrations, click on 'API' to copy your Castos APO Token:
    ​

  4. Navigate back to the Alitu screen and paste the Castos API Token into the box, then click 'Continue':
    ​

  5. You should then see the note below that your integration was successful:


    ​

  6. Now, if you click on the Publish page for the episode you want to publish, you can click on 'Send to Castos' to publish your episode to that account:
    ​

  7. On the next screen you will select which podcast (if you have more than one) in your Castos account that you'd like to publish to and click 'Send to Castos and Publish':
    ​

  8. You should now see that your episode has been published to your host:
