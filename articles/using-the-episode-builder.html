Building your episode


The Episode Builder makes it easy to create your episodes. You can:

Add your files

  • Add multiple files ie. voice clips, music, ads etc by clicking the + icon

Move the files around

  • Drag and drop clips by clicking over the clip and then dragging anywhere in the timeline

Add an Episode Description

  • add an episode description and id3 tags in the ‘Episode Details’ section

Add Fades and Transitions between clips

  • add fades and transitions to your sections

edit, rename or remove clips




Duplicating, copying and renaming blocks

Each block in the Episode Builder has a More Options ( … ) menu. To access the menu, click the '…' icon.
​
​


​
This menu lets you:

  • Rename the block

  • Duplicate the block within the same episode

  • Copy the block to another episode by clicking 'Copy to'. A pop-up will appear where you can choose which episode you want to copy the block to. You can also choose where the block should be placed in the episode, such as at the beginning or at the end.
    ​


    ​
    When you duplicate or copy a block, all edits are preserved.
    ​
    ​

Episode Preview

  • Preview your entire episode using the playback bar at the bottom of the Episode Builder Screen
    ​

You can also split clips inside the Audio Editor when you click the ‘Edit’ button.

When you're done with your episode, click the 'Build my episode' button at the top right corner.
