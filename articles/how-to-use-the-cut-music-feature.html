The Cut Music feature in Alitu lets you set the exact length of the intro and outro music you will use in your podcast.

Watch this quick tutorial on how to use this new feature:

​

1. Click the ‘Length’ option inside the Episode Builder

2. From the pop-up window, you’ll see details of your music clip such as the 1. original length, 2. your preferred length, 3. time input box where you type in your preferred length in minutes and seconds, 4. - and + controllers where you can easily decrease or increase the length by 1 second/minute or by 10 seconds/minutes, 5. 'Reset length' option if you want to start the process.

3. Click the X button when you’re done with the setup.
