Once your episode is complete, you can publish the episode directly to your Buzzsprout account. You can decide whether or not you want your episode published as a draft or as a live episode.

Integrate your Alitu Account with your Buzzsprout Account

  1. On the publish page, click 'Set up my Show':
    ​

  2. Click 'I use Buzzsprout':
    ​

  3. On the next screen you will need to add your Buzzsprout Podcast ID and your Buzzsprout API Token. We recommend you open your Buzzsprout account in another browser because you will find both of these keys in the Settings >> API section of your Buzzsprout account:


    ​

  4. Inside Buzzsprout, navigate to Settings >> My Profile, then click on API Key to find your Podcast ID and API Token:
    ​

  5. Once you've added your Buzzsprout credentials to Alitu, click 'Continue':
    ​

  6. On the Publish page you can now click 'Send to Buzzsprout'':
    ​

  7. Choose between 'Leave unpublished' and 'Publish immediately' and then click 'Send to Buzzsprout'
    ​

That's it. Your episode will be uploaded directly to your Buzzsprout account.
