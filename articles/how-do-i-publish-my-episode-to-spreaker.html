Once your episode is complete, you can publish the episode directly to your Spreaker account.
​

It's simplest if you sign into your Spreaker account in another browser before you begin this setup.


​

Integrate your Alitu Account with your Spreaker Account

  1. On the publish page, click the Set up my show button:

  2. Choose I use Spreaker from the pop-up window

  3. Log into your Spreaker account

    If you're already logged into Spreaker, you will see an authorization window and can just click 'Authorize':

  4. On your publish page, you can now click Send to Spreaker:


    ​

  5. Select your show on Spreaker then click Send to Spreaker and Publish:

That's it. Your episode has just been published to your show on Spreaker. You can visit your Spreaker dashboard to make any edits that you'd like to the episode settings.
