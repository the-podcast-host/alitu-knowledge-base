What are downloads?

"Download numbers are one of the most obvious metrics for measuring the success of your podcast. They are immediately accessible to you, from the minute you launch your first episode. Watching the numbers climb can be rewarding. Some might even say addictive." - Matthew from The Podcast Host

Downloads are counted when someone listens to your podcast episode via your website (embedded players) or podcast apps.

How to access your Podcast Statistics

You will only have access to the statistics information if you are using Alitu Hosting. If you host with an externally, then your statistics will be viewable through their platform.

Click on the statistics link in the left navigation bar to view the statistics for your Alitu-hosted podcast. You must have published a live episode for the statistics to show up, scheduling does not create statistics.

Total Podcast Downloads

This section gives a summary of your downloads on a certain period:

  • Today

  • Yesterday

  • Last 7 days

  • Last 30 days

  • Last 90 days

  • All-time

Average Podcast Downloads

This section shows the average downloads per day, per week, and per month based on a certain period. You can use the dropdown tool to select your preferred date range.

Episode Downloads Overview

This shows a breakdown of your episode downloads. You can select from the dropdown your preferred number of episodes to view

Downloads per Country

This shows which country your downloads are coming from. The report is currently showing ‘All-time’ date range.

Listening Apps

This shows which app listeners are using.

Devices

This section shows the percentage of what device your listeners used - mobile vs computer. The report is currently showing ‘All-time’ date range.

Operating Systems

The OS used to download your podcast episodes. The report is currently showing ‘All-time’ date range. ‘Others’ are downloads from less recognized third-party apps.
