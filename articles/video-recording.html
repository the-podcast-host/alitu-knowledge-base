Introduction

You can now record video in the recording studio.

The video recording will capture the best quality available from your camera, up to 1080p (HD).

There are no video editing tools available in Alitu at this time, so If you wish to edit the downloaded video file you can import it into any video editing software.

There are many video editing solutions on the market and which is best for your will depend on your needs and budget:

  • ClipChamp (Microsoft)

  • Adobe Premier Pro

  • Camtasia

  • CapCut

How to Record Video

To record your video when using the studio all you need to do is select upon entry to your studio whether you want to record audio only or video and audio.

If you select "Audio & Video" then Alitu will record and create a video file in your library once you have completed recording.

How to Download Your Recorded Video

When you have finished recording, the video output will be available to download from your "My Library" page. Hover over the date and time, and the download icon will appear. Click on it, and you'll see the two download options, 'Video or Audio'

For further guidance on how to use the recording studio please see our dedicated guide on recording in the studio
