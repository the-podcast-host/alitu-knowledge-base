Alitu Hosting provides detailed analytics to all subscribers. You can:

✅ Keep track of your downloads per episode, and total downloads

✅ Find out where your listeners are from, and what devices they listen on

✅ Give sponsors the numbers they need to invest in your show

If you want to use a 3rd-party service, like Chartable, to get even more detailed analytics, you can do that with Alitu.

  1. Navigate to 'Your Podcast Details'

  2. Scroll down to 'Stats URL prefix (Optional)'

  3. Past your 3rd-party URL prefix into the box

  4. Click 'Save Changes' at the bottom of the screen

That's it! Your 3rd-party tracking has been added to your Alitu RSS Feed.

Don't hesitate to reach out to us via email or chat if you have any other questions.
