Transcription Fair Use Limit

Each Alitu account has a fair usage limit of 12 hours of transcription per month. You will see an error message if you exceed the limit.

You can view your transcription usage in the "settings" section of your account.

If you need more transcription hours, the support team can manually process this for you. The cost will be $1/hr. All you need to do is contact the support team using the chat box icon in the bottom right and let them know how many more hours you need.
