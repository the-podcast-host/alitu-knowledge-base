Why you should clear your cache and cookies

Clearing your cache and cookies can solve the following problems:

  1. Episodes not loading

  2. Blank screen

  3. Error messages

Clearing your cache also ensures that your browser has the latest Alitu updates.

How to clear cache in Chrome

In the top left corner of the Chrome menu, click 'Delete Browsing Data':

Then follow these steps:

  1. Select the time range depending on how much cache you want to clear

  2. Select the cookies and other site data option

  3. Select the Cached images and files option

  4. Click the 'Delete data' button to clear your cache & cookies

Alternative method to clear cache

1. Open the Alitu app website https://app.alitu.com/ or login to your Alitu account.

2. Press the right key from your mouse (right click) and then choose ‘Inspect’ if you’re on MacOS or ‘Inspect Element’ for Windows OS.

3. Right-click on the ‘Reload’ icon and then choose ‘Empty Cache and Hard Reload’.

You’re good to go!
