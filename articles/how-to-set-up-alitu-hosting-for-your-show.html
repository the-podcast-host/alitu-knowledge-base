This article is not for podcasters who are migrating their podcasts to Alitu from another host. This is for podcasts that have never been hosted anywhere before.

If you are hosting with a third-party service then you will complete your podcast description, etc. on their site not on Alitu.

Select Alitu hosting:

In your Alitu dashboard, navigate to Show Settings > Show Hosting and select Alitu as your podcast host: 'Go live with Alitu for free'.
​

Enter your Show Details

  • Fill in your Show name and Show description.

    Add your Show Cover Art:

  • Upload your podcast artwork it should be a 3000 x 3000 .jpeg or .png file, no larger than 5MB.

Add your Show Creator Information:

  • Once your artwork has been uploaded, add the Show creator's name and email

Add your Show Category(s):

  • First choose the Main category that your podcast will be listed under. Choose the category that best fits your show. You can then add optional secondary and tertiary categories. We use the official Apple podcast categories to make sure your podcast will be accepted into as many podcast directories as possible. Also choose the primary language spoken on your show

Add additional details:

  • Next, fill in details including the Show language, format, rating, time zone and locking your feed from transfer

Add Podcasting 2.0 details:

  • Finally, fill in details for Podcasting 2.0 features (optional) including Show location, publication frequency, funding link, license, and custom text which you want added to your RSS feed.

Once you've finished adding all your details, click 'Save'.
​

Set up your Show Website

If you want to use the free Alitu Show website, visit the 'Show Website' settings. You will need to have completed your show details before doing this.

  1. Enter a subdomain for your show. This subdomain will be followed by alitu.com so it will look something like this, https://subdomain.alitu.com where your actual subdomain will show in place of the word, subdomain. Click the 'Claim' button.

  2. It will take about 24 hours for your subdomain to become active, so please don't make any changes to this subdomain once you have set it or you will reset the clock.

  3. If your show is new and you don't have links to your Apple, Google, and Spotify pages yet, skip these for now.

  4. You can continue filling in the social media links, as well as a link to your personal website if you like, and then click 'Save settings'.

  5. Then, add your Website Footer Text which is usually your name or the name of your company, and click, 'Save settings'.

Set up Show Distribution

To set up distribution make sure you have published at least one episode of your podcast to your new Alitu hosting account.

You DO NOT need to set up distribution if you have moved from another podcast media host to Alitu, instead set up a 301 redirect

Navigate to the 'Show Distribution' section of your settings so you can start sharing your podcast with the world.

  1. You will find your Podcast RSS feed here in this section. You can easily copy the RSS feed anytime you need it.

  2. Now you can copy your Podcast RSS Feed URL and submit your show to both Apple Podcasts and Spotify. Clicking either button takes you directly to the website where you submit your podcast to the directories.

  3. Here is a guide on distributing your podcast to other directories

You will only use the 'Redirect Podcast RSS Feed' if you are moving your podcast AWAY from Alitu. Otherwise, you can ignore this setting.


That's it! You have successfully set up your podcast hosting here at Alitu.

After you hear back from Apple and Spotify, you can return to the Podcast Website Settings page to add direct links to your podcast on those directories.
