Ways to publish your episodes

Once you have finished editing your podcast episode in Alitu, you have three choices for publishing:

  • Download your file so that you can upload the final file anywhere you choose

  • Publish your episode directly to your podcast host, either as a draft or a final episode

  • Create a video from your podcast episode to share on video sites or social media


Download to Publish Manually

If you want to download your episode, click the 'Download as mp3' button and your finished episode will download to your computer.

Publish Directly to an Integrated Podcast Host

To publish your episode directly to one of our integrated podcast hosts, click the 'Set up my Show' button and follow the guide here to set up your hosting:

Managing your hosting

Once your show hosting is set up, click the 'Send to' button. In this example the account is integrated with Buzzsprout.
