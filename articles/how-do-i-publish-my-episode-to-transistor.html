Once your podcast episode is complete and has been exported to apply all the changes, you can publish the episode directly to your Transistor hosting account.
​

Integrate your Alitu account with your Transistor account

  1. On the publish page, click 'Set up my show':
    ​

  2. Click 'I use Transistor':
    ​

  3. Navigate to your Transistor account and click the down arrow next to your name in the upper right corner to select 'Your Account':
    ​

  4. On the Account screen scroll down to the API Access section and copy the API Key from the box, as shown:
    ​

  5. Paste this Transistor API key into the box in Alitu, then click 'Continue'
    ​

  6. Click 'Send to Transistor'
    ​

  7. On the next screen you will select which podcast (if you have more than one) in your Transistor account that you'd like to publish to and click 'Send to Transistor as a draft' or 'Send to Transistor and publish':


    ​

  8. You should now see that your episode has been sent to Transistor:


    ​

Please let us know if you have any questions and we'll be happy to help!
