Please note: If you are currently on your free trial you can only create a maximum of 4 episodes.

Add an Episode

To start, click the 'Add an Episode' button at the top of the screen. You can also create a new episode by clicking the "+" icon in the sidebar.

Add your Audio Files

There are 4 ways to add files to your episode on this screen:

  1. Drag and drop audio files here from your device.

  2. Select 'Click to upload' to manually upload audio files from your device.

  3. Choose 'Record Something' to record a new file right now.

  4. Select 'Add from Library' to pick a recording saved in your library.

When you've added your audio files, click 'Continue':

You're Ready to Edit

Once your files have been uploaded, you will land on the Episode Builder to begin editing your episode.

Preview Your Episode

You can easily preview your full episode by using the playback bar at the bottom of the episode builder screen.
