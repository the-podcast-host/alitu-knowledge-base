What is Alitu

Alitu is a software solution that is designed to be easy to use and to make it simple to establish a podcast workflow that suits you. It is a great solution when starting your podcast journey and starting to create and build your podcast episodes. Let's think about creating your episode in terms of the flow of the podcast timeline.

Creating your Show

This is where you will create, build and see all your episodes and their status. From here, you can also choose to playback, edit, or publish your episode.

  • Intro & Outro

    You can add music anywhere in your episode, but many podcasters will add intro music at the beginning of their episode. Upload your own music or use music from our free library.

  • The Episode File(s)

    This is where you will upload your episode audio into Alitu. If you have more than one file you can merge them when you upload as long as they are the exact same length. When you upload your audio, Alitu processes it for optimum sound and adds the newly polished audio to your episode editor and library.

  • Editing the Main Episode

    In the Episode Builder correct any mistakes, take out portions you don't want, and edit your podcast with the easy-to-use tools, including the removal of filler words like uhms and ahs, editing via text, insert background music and a whole host of other smart editing tools.

  • Publishing the Episode

    Publishing the episode creates a file that is ready to be shared with your audience. If you listen to the 'final' version and want to make any changes you can go back and edit again, then re-process the episode.

    At the publishing stage, you can decide whether to publish directly to your podcast host, download the audio file, or create a video version of your episode. Or you can do all three!

Merge tool

You can easily merge two files (they must be the exact same length) when you're uploading them to Alitu. This is ideal if you use something like Zoom to record your podcast interviews and you end up with 2 files, 1 for each voice.

Split tool

This is handy if you want to split a recording and add ads and fx in. It's also helpful if you want to edit out and cover a mistake.

Magic Filters

A tool available in the editor to make it easier and quicker to edit your audio. Will remove filler words such as ahs and ums and also remove silences.

Studio (Recording)

You can record online interviews with up to 10 guests right inside Alitu. You will be able to record audio and Video in the your Recording Studio. Video download is available from your "My Library"

Clip Genie

The Clip Genie is the best place to add background music to your voice clips.

My Library

The Library is where all your files are added after upload or recording. You can playback or delete your files here.

Alitu Hosting

Alitu hosting is where you can update your podcast settings, grab your RSS feed, have your own podcast website and submit your podcast to directories.
