Step-by-Step Guide

You can easily record with your guests, have it processed to sound awesome, and edit your episode right inside Alitu.

Note: Before getting started, please be sure to check the best practices when using the Recording Studio.

Enter "Your Recording Studio" via the record button in the left hand menu.

Get your studio link to send to guests

  1. Grab your studio link by clicking on the 'Record' icon in your left navigation bar. Select "Invite Other" and copy your studio link which is unique to your account. Easily send ahead of time when scheduling recordings with your guests.

Allow Microphone & Camera Permissions

  1. When you enter the recording studio, your will likely see a pop-up asking you to give your browser access to your microphone. Please click 'Allow on every visit' to continue. Your guests on their end will also be prompted to give this permission.
    ​

  2. Select the microphone and camera you want to use and choose whether you want to record audio only or audio and video.

  3. Click 'More settings' to choose the headphones you are going to listen via.

  4. When everything’s set, enter the recording studio. Please note that your guest won’t be able to join the call without you in the studio. Best practice is to enter your studio a few minutes earlier than your guest.

  5. Here’s what the recording studio looks like when you enter initially.

Starting your Call

The audio WILL NOT automatically start recording upon entering the studio. Remember to press the 'Start recording' button when you and your guests are ready.

  1. Click the start recording button to start recording the call when you and your guests are ready.

  2. Chat box. You can send messages or important links here. Messages won’t be saved so please make sure to copy any important messages.

  3. This is you. Your guests will appear as other small windows - similar to Zoom or Google Meet. Check out our recording best practices to let your guests know so you have the best recording possible.

  4. Settings. You can update your mic and headphone by clicking this button.

  5. This is where you can copy your studio link if you want to share it with more guests.

  6. Video button to enable or disable video feed.

  7. Mute. You can mute and unmute your end during the call. Note: You won’t be able to mute or unmute your guest’s end.

  8. Sound indicator

Pausing your Recording

You can pause the recording during the Studio session.

Click Resume recording when you are ready to start recording again.

Ending your Call

When you are finished recording hit "Stop Recording" to end the recording. You can continue to chat with your guests after this.

If you are completely finished you can then leave the studio.
​

Your recording will be saved into your "My Library" page. If you chose to record the video, then that video file will also be available to download via your library.
